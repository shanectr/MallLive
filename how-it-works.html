How It Works

Sell How You Want

MallLIVE transforms a traditional department store into a series of dynamic live studios where businesses and creators can rent space to sell products via TikTok Shop.

As a first-of-its-kind concept, MallLIVE bridges the gap between physical retail and digital commerce, offering a new way for brands to connect with consumers in real time.

The rise of live shopping and social commerce, particularly through platforms like TikTok, presents a lucrative opportunity. MallLIVE capitalizes on this trend by offering brands an interactive and immersive way to engage customers, increasing sales and customer loyalty.
